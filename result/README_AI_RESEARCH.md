## 💡 Core Insights

### What's Changing
1. **From Scale to Specialization**: Moving away from massive models to domain-specific, efficient ones
2. **From Tools to Agents**: AI shifting from passive tools to autonomous agents
3. **From Hype to Results**: Emphasis on measurable ROI and real business outcomes
4. **From Cloud to Edge**: Increasing on-device deployment and privacy-focused solutions
5. **From Unregulated to Compliant**: Governance and compliance becoming mandatory

### What's NOT Happening (Yet)
- ❌ No GPT-5 class model released
- ❌ Personal AI assistants not mainstream
- ❌ New form factors replacing smartphones
- ❌ Universal AI safety solution
